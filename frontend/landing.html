About VAANI

Built for privacy. Built for everyone.

VAANI isn't just an app; it's a movement to make communication a universal right. We believe that accessibility tools shouldn't rely on expensive cloud servers or high-speed internet.

By leveraging cutting-edge Edge AI (MediaPipe) and running entirely locally on your device, VAANI guarantees total privacy and zero latency. It's offline, it's free, and it's designed specifically for the unique nuances of Indian Sign Language.
